Erinnerung Ableitungsregeln

  • Konstante Funktion: \(\operatorname{y}(x) = c\) => \(\dfrac{\operatorname{d} \operatorname{y}}{\operatorname{d} x} = 0\)
  • Konstanter Faktor: \(\operatorname{y}(x) = c \cdot \operatorname{v}(x)\) => \(\dfrac{\operatorname{d} \operatorname{y}}{\operatorname{d} x} = c \cdot \dfrac{\operatorname{d} \operatorname{v}}{\operatorname{d} x}\)
  • Summe/Differenz: \(\operatorname{y}(x) = \operatorname{u}(x) \pm \operatorname{v}(x)\) => \(\dfrac{\operatorname{d} \operatorname{y}}{\operatorname{d} x} = \dfrac{\operatorname{d} \operatorname{u}}{\operatorname{d} x} \pm \dfrac{\operatorname{d} \operatorname{v}}{\operatorname{d} x}\)
  • Produkt: \(\operatorname{y}(x) = \operatorname{u}(x) \cdot \operatorname{v}(x)\) => \(\dfrac{\operatorname{d} \operatorname{y}}{\operatorname{d} x} = \dfrac{\operatorname{d} \operatorname{u}}{\operatorname{d} x} \cdot \operatorname{v}(x) + \operatorname{u}(x) \cdot \dfrac{\operatorname{d} \operatorname{v}}{\operatorname{d} x}\)
  • Kettenregel: \(\operatorname{y}(x) = \operatorname{u}(\operatorname{v}(x))\) => \(\dfrac{\operatorname{d} y}{\operatorname{d} x} = \dfrac{\operatorname{d} y}{\operatorname{d} z} \cdot \dfrac{\operatorname{d} z}{\operatorname{d} x}\) mit \(\operatorname{z} = \operatorname{v}(x)\) und \(\operatorname{y} = \operatorname{u}(\operatorname{z})\)
  • Partielle Ableitung: \(\nabla \operatorname{y}(\mathbf{w}) = \left( \dfrac{\partial \operatorname{y}}{\partial w_{0}}, \dfrac{\partial \operatorname{y}}{\partial w_{1}}, \ldots, \dfrac{\partial \operatorname{y}}{\partial w_{n}} \right)^T\)

Tafel: Beispiele

Nomenklatur MLP


  • Aktivierung Neuron \(j\): \(a_j = \sum_i w_{ji} z_i\)

  • Ausgabe von Neuron \(j\): \(z_j = g(a_j)\)

  • Transferfunktion: \(g(a) = 1/(1+\exp(-a))\)

  • Ableitung: \(g'(a) = g(a)(1-g(a))\)

Nomenklatur MLP: Schichten


  • Ausgabe-Schicht: Index \(k\): \(y_k = z_k = g(a_k) = g(\sum_j w_{kj} z_j)\)
  • Hidden Layer: Index \(j\): \(z_j = g(a_j) = g(\sum_i w_{ji} z_i)\)

  • Eingabe-Schicht: Index \(i\): \(z_i = x_i\)

  • \(w_{kj}\): Gewicht gehÃ¶rt zu Neuron \(k\), Verbindung zu Neuron \(j\)

\[ \begin{array}{lll} \dfrac{\partial E}{\partial w_{i}} & = & \dfrac{\partial}{\partial w_{i}} \sum_d E^{(d)} = \sum_d \dfrac{\partial E^{(d)}}{\partial w_{i}} = \sum_d \dfrac{\partial}{\partial w_{i}} \dfrac{1}{2}(y^{(d)} - t^{(d)})^{2} \\[11pt] & = & \sum_d \dfrac{1}{2} \dfrac{\partial}{\partial a^{(d)}} (g(a^{(d)}) - t^{(d)})^{2} \cdot \dfrac{\partial a^{(d)}}{\partial w_{i}} \\[11pt] & = & \sum_d (g(a^{(d)}) - t^{(d)}) \cdot \dfrac{\partial}{\partial a^{(d)}} (g(a^{(d)}) - t^{(d)}) \cdot x_{i}^{(d)} \\[22pt] & = & \sum_{d} \underbrace{(y^{(d)} - t^{(d)}) \cdot g'}_{\displaystyle\delta^{(d)}} \cdot x_{i}^{(d)} \end{array} \]

\(\Delta w_i^{(d)} = -\eta\dfrac{\partial E^{(d)}}{\partial w_{i}} = -\eta\delta^{(d)} x_i^{(d)}\)

\(\Delta w_i^{(d)} = -\eta\delta^{(d)} x_i^{(d)} \qquad \text{ mit } \;\;\; \delta^{(d)} = (y^{(d)} - t^{(d)}) \cdot g'\)
